Privacy Policy

Welcome to Wimson! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Wimson, we may collect the following types of personal and non-personal data:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, billing details, payment information, and any information you provide when contacting us.
  • Account Information:Order history, and preferences.
  • Transaction Data: Records of your purchases, subscriptions, and interactions with our services.
  • Device and Usage Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and pages visited.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies to enhance user experience and track website activity.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our services.
  • To process payments and transactions securely.
  • To personalize your user experience and recommend content.
  • To send promotional emails, updates, and customer support messages.
  • To prevent fraudulent activity and ensure website security.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. How We Share Your Information

Wimson does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. However, we may share data with:

  • Payment Processors: Your financial data is securely processed by third-party payment providers.
  • Service Providers: We may share data with vendors who assist in email delivery, analytics, and customer support.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, we may disclose information to authorities.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy. This includes fulfilling legal, tax, and regulatory obligations.

5. How We Protect Your Information

We implement strict security measures, including encryption, access controls, and secure payment gateways. However, no system is completely secure, and we advise users to protect their account credentials.
